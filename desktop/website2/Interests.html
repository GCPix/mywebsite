Music

I enjoy a lot of different things but to be honest have little time for most of them now. I have played a few different musical instruments but the main one was the accordion. An accordion is:

a musical instrument played by stretching and squeezing with the hands to work a central bellows that blows air over metal reeds, the melody and chords being sounded by buttons or keys.

I would also add that the standard adult one is heavy so, in case anyone was wondering, no I do not play standing up. I also don't play Scottish (unless someone makes me). I have competed since I was about 8 and reached semi-professional level but it takes a huge commitment to continue at that level including competing around the world and simply rehearsal time so I hung up my hat afte the Virtuoso. I didn't stop playing, just not at that level. I was part of a music school in Paisley, taught by Graham Laurie and part of his orchestra.
