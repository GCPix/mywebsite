Hello I am Pixie

Probably not who you were expecting but the human said she needed help and I have agreed in exchange for treats and pets on demand. I am sure you would rather read more about me but I am told she should show first. If you want to know more about my life click on my picture to see my own page.

The human in question calls herself Gillian. She has been my servant for 8 years since they ripped me from my mother. I should say my main servant, there are two others in my kingdom but they don't seem to do a lot

So this is her. I didn't know her when this was taken, she must be really quite old as she said this was 20 years ago!

Gillian has worked for a number of years as a trainer, she can't be very good at it she was never able to train me but apparently other humans thnk she is competent

Before becoming a trainer she was a Chemical Engineer, working in the food industry. She worked for a company called Campbell's Grocery Products (no family relation) where she ran a pilot plant and ran 'Thermal Processing checks' which was just a technical way of saying she made sure food was safe to eat. Apparently the food wasn't fit for me to eat but she claims it was quite nice for humans.
